Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-09

Digital services: Refund eligibility depends on delivery status and time spent.

1) When a Refund May Be Approved

  • Non-delivery: If we cannot deliver the purchased service within a reasonable time after payment confirmation.
  • Duplicate payment: If you accidentally paid twice for the same order, we will refund the duplicate amount.
  • Service not started: If you request cancellation before any work begins (and before resources are reserved), we may approve a full refund.
  • Technical failure: If a confirmed order cannot be completed due to a technical issue on our side.

2) When Refunds Are Usually Not Approved

  • Delivered orders: If the service has been delivered as described.
  • Change of mind: If you simply change your mind after work has started.
  • Missing customer details: If delivery is delayed because required information was not provided.
  • Abuse or prohibited requests: Orders involving illegal activity, fraud, or abuse may be refused; refunds (if any) depend on the situation and time spent.

3) Partial Refunds

If work has started, we may offer a partial refund depending on time spent, reserved resources, or completed milestones.

4) How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact support via WhatsApp or email.
  2. Include your Order ID, package name, and reason for the request.
  3. We may ask for additional details to verify the request.

5) Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method via Stripe. Processing time may depend on your bank/card provider.

6) Chargebacks

If you open a chargeback without contacting support first, we may pause support on the order while the dispute is handled. Please contact us first so we can resolve the issue quickly.
